The App does not collect any personal information from you directly.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

These services are:

AdMob may collect information about your device, such as your advertising ID and device identifier, to serve you with targeted advertising. You can learn more about AdMob's privacy practices here: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en

Google Play Game Services may collect information about your account, such as your username and profile picture. You can learn more about Google Play Game Services' privacy practices here: https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/11416267?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid
